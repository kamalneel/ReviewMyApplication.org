## Key Differentiators Discovered

### Stanford University
- **Signature criterion**: Intellectual vitality - love of learning for itself
- **Unique essay**: "What matters to you and why?"
- **Culture**: Silicon Valley innovation and entrepreneurship
- **Essays**: Weighted exceptionally heavily

### Yale University
- **Signature criterion**: Humanities-driven intellectual curiosity
- **Unique feature**: Residential colleges + shopping period
- **Testing**: Reinstated requirement (SAT/ACT)
- **Culture**: Liberal arts ideal and broad intellectual engagement

### Princeton University
- **Signature criterion**: Independent thinking and research potential
- **Unique feature**: Undergraduate focus (no professional schools)
- **Requirements**: Independent work (junior papers, senior thesis)
- **System**: Precept discussion-based learning

### Columbia University
- **Signature criterion**: Readiness for Core Curriculum
- **Unique feature**: Intensive Core Curriculum + NYC integration
- **Testing**: Test-flexible (SAT, ACT, or 3 AP scores)
- **Culture**: Urban engagement + seminar discussions

### University of Pennsylvania
- **Signature criterion**: Interdisciplinary thinking + practical application
- **Unique feature**: 4 undergraduate schools (College, Wharton, Engineering, Nursing)
- **Culture**: "Theory and practice" - pre-professional focus
- **Demonstrated interest**: Actually matters (unlike most Ivies)

### Duke University
- **Signature criterion**: Connecting learning with purpose
- **Unique feature**: DukeEngage + service orientation
- **Culture**: "Knowledge in service to society"
- **Programs**: Bass Connections for interdisciplinary research

### University of Chicago
- **Signature criterion**: Love of learning for its own sake - "life of the mind"
- **Unique feature**: Legendary quirky essay prompts
- **Requirements**: Rigorous Core Curriculum
- **Culture**: Intellectual intensity + playfulness ("Where fun comes to die")
- **Essay weight**: Most important part of application

### Northwestern University
- **Signature criterion**: Clear passions + purpose
- **Unique feature**: 6 undergraduate schools (including Medill Journalism, Bienen Music)
- **System**: Quarter system (10-week quarters)
- **Culture**: Academic rigor + Big Ten spirit + pre-professional focus
- **Location**: Evanston (Chicago access) + urban-campus balance
